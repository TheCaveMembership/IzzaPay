Izza Pay is a seamless solution for merchants to accept Pi Coins on their Shopify stores.

Merchants can look forward to:

  • Easy integration with Shopify
  • Secure transactions through the Pi Browser
  • Setting their own exchange rates
  • Receiving payments directly into their Pi Network wallets
  • A user-friendly dashboard for managing transactions

Stay tuned for more updates! For inquiries, contact us at support@izzapay.com.
